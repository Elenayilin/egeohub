Geography Resources for Victorian Secondary Students

Welcome to eGeoHub – your go-to site for Geography learning materials and practice.

VCE Geography (Units 1–4)

Unit 1: Hazards and Disasters

Study natural and human-induced hazards and evaluate responses to disaster events.

Unit 2: Tourism: Issues and Challenges

Explore patterns, impacts, and sustainable management of tourism globally and locally.

Unit 3: Changing the Land

Investigate land cover and land use changes, and evaluate their impacts and responses.

Unit 4: Human Population Trends and Issues

Analyse global population dynamics and evaluate responses to population challenges.

Years 7–10 Geography

Year 7: Landscapes and Landforms

Examine natural landscapes, processes shaping them, and their management.

Year 8: Urbanisation

Understand urban growth patterns, livability, and sustainable urban futures.

Year 9: Biomes and Food Security

Study the distribution of biomes, food production, and challenges for food security.

Year 10: Environmental Change

Analyse environmental change and evaluate responses at different scales.
